# zh-TW FPS Terms

Use this reference only when the source content is clearly FPS, battle royale, or hero shooter content.

## Core Rule

- Translate to player-sounding Taiwan wording, not textbook Chinese.
- Prefer official Taiwan game names and UI terms when they exist.
- Taiwan players often mix Chinese with stable English callouts. Keep the English term if that sounds more natural than a forced literal translation.
- Very short comms are preferred in combat callouts.

## Style Notes

- `爆頭`, `壓槍`, `準星`, `清角`, `架點` are preferred over literal mainland-style wording.
- Do not force-translate comms like `peek`, `lurk`, `clutch`, `flick` if the mixed English reads more like a real Taiwan player.
- Damage-state calls should stay terse: `破甲`, `打到肉`, `他很殘`, `拉人`, `先退`, `轉點`, `包夾`.
- For hero shooters, use the official Taiwan skill, role, and map terms if they are established in the game client.
